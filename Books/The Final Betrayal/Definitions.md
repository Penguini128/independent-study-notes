**Coup d'état** - an illegal and overt attempt by a military organization or other government elites to unseat an incumbent person or leadership. 

[**Dark Enlightenment**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_Enlightenment) - A reactionary philosophical and political movement against Enlightenment values, in favor of a return to traditional societal constructions and forms of government, such as absolute monarchism.

Protégé - one who is protected or trained or whose career is furthered by a person of experience, prominence, or influence
